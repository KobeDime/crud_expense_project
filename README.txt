# KobeSpendWise

KobeSpendWise is a simple yet efficient budget calculator designed to help you effortlessly manage your expenses. Built with HTML, CSS, PHP, and MySQL, this application allows you to track your spending, maintain a budget, and gain valuable insights into your financial habits.

## Project Structure

- **`index.php`**:
  - The main user interface for KobeSpendWise. This file enables users to input their income, expenses, and view their current balance. It serves as the entry point to the application.

- **`process.php`**:
  - Handles all backend operations, including creating, reading, updating, and deleting (CRUD) budget entries. This file processes user inputs and updates the budget data accordingly.

## Features

### 1. Income Management
- **Add & Manage Income Sources:** Easily input and manage various income sources to keep track of all your earnings.

### 2. Expense Tracking
- **Record & Categorize Expenses:** Log your expenses with categories for better tracking and analysis.

### 3. Balance Calculation
- **Automatic Balance Calculation:** The application automatically calculates your balance based on the difference between your income and expenses.

### 4. CRUD Operations
- **Full CRUD Support:** Perform Create, Read, Update, and Delete operations for both income and expense entries, allowing you to maintain accurate records.

### 5. User-Friendly Interface
- **Simple & Intuitive Design:** Navigate the application with ease, thanks to its clean and straightforward user interface.

## Usage

### 1. Add Income
- **Enter Income Sources:** Input your various income sources to track your total earnings.

### 2. Add Expenses
- **Enter & Categorize Expenses:** Log your expenses, categorizing them for better financial management.

### 3. View Balance
- **Check Your Balance:** Your current balance is displayed, reflecting the difference between your income and expenses.

### 4. Modify or Delete Entries
- **Update or Delete Records:** Easily modify or remove any income or expense entries as needed to keep your budget up to date.

## Technologies Used

- **Frontend:** HTML, CSS
- **Backend:** PHP
- **Database:** MySQL
